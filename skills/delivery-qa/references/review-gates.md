# Delivery QA Review Gates

Use this file to keep Step 20 and Step 23 separate.

## Step 20: failure-analysis review

Check whether:

- the failing case is identified clearly
- the evidence is sufficient
- the root-cause category is justified
- the analysis should pass or be redone

## Step 23: fix-plan review

Check whether:

- the routed owner and touched scope are correct
- the fix tasks are concrete enough to execute
- sequencing or dependency risks are called out
- the plan should pass or be revised

## Non-negotiable rule

These two gates always produce different review files.
